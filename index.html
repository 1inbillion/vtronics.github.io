Evaluations are completed within 24 hours after confirmation. But, the Repair time can vary based on our workload and availability of parts. Our standard turnaround time is just 1-5 days. We also offer a rush service for those critical times when you need your repair back faster.

Please note any units with liquid damage may take longer than this timeframe.

I purchased my device overseas. Can you repair it?

Yes, in most cases we can repair devices from overseas (some exceptions apply).  Note that the parts may take longer to arrive for products bought overseas.
